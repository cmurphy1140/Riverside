Life on the Lagoon

More than a restaurant — it's a way of life

Nestled along the scenic Indian River Lagoon in Vero Beach, Florida, Riverside Cafe has become a beloved gathering place for locals and visitors alike. From the moment you arrive — whether by car or by boat — you'll feel the laid-back, waterfront atmosphere that sets us apart.

What started as a simple idea — great food with a great view — has grown into one of the Treasure Coast's favorite dining destinations. Over the years, we've built a reputation for fresh seafood, an electric sports bar experience, and some of the most stunning sunset views on the east coast of Florida.

Today, Riverside Cafe is where friends gather to watch the game on one of our 35 HD TVs, families come for Sunday Brunch with endless mimosas, and boaters dock up for an afternoon on the water. It's all part of the Riverside experience — and we wouldn't have it any other way.

What We're Known For

Five reasons Riverside Cafe is Vero Beach's favorite

The Cuisine

From comfy finger foods and classic sandwiches to intimate semi-formal entrees of fresh fish and steaks — our menu has something for every occasion and every appetite.

The Bar

Our premier bar features draft beer chilled to a frosty 33 degrees and professional mixologists crafting cocktails. It's the best seat in the house.

The Views

Spectacular sunset vistas across the Indian River Lagoon make every meal a special occasion. There's nothing quite like dinner with a front-row seat to nature's show.

The Sports

With 35 large HD TVs, BOSE surround sound, and Direct TV NFL Sunday Ticket and College Football packages, you'll never miss a moment of the action.

The Docking

Arrive by boat and enjoy complimentary guest docking while you dine. We also offer monthly dock space rentals — making Riverside your regular waterfront stop.
